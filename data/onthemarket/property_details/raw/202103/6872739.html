Property description

Quickly rising in the heart of Vauxhall, Keybridge development offers this fabulous two bedroom apartment in Keybridge, SW8.

The development offers a range of new apartments from studio to three-bedroom in the UK’s largest brick tower with 441 new homes being offered in the 37-storey building.

These new apartments benefit from stylish new kitchens, designed for each flat individually and fitted with the latest, top-of-the-range appliances. The timber flooring and underfloor heating provide warmth and aesthetic throughout the entire apartment covering the living room, dining room, kitchen and hallways. The bathrooms, many of which are ensuite are fitted with quality appliances and also finished with stunning porcelain tiles.

Residents benefit from a 24-hour concierge service easy lift access, a Club Lounge to hold meetings and provide reading space as well as hot desks and high-speed Wi-Fi. Residents also have access to a private spa and gym which includes an indoor pool, sauna, steam room and changing facilities. There is also a dedicated space for secure cycle storage below the development.

At the heart of Vauxhall, Keybridge is extremely well-connected located just a short, six-minute walk from the Zone 1 Vauxhall Underground Station which is on the Victoria line, giving you fast and efficient transport links to the rest of London. There are also a handful of excellent schools and universities in the local area as well as superb restaurants, shops, nightlife and other amenities to explore.
